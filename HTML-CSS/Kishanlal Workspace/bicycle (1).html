REGISTRATION

Welcome to TempTravel. Users can register themselves and create their own account where they can access to all the feature of TempTravel.

BOOK

Done with the registration? Let us move towards booking. Click on BOOK to continue smoothly with further process

CHOICE

From the list of options, choose a vehicle of your convenience. You will then be directed to a list of rentals available for that vehicle. Choose an option according to your preference.

FINAL BOOKING

Almost done! Click on BOOK NOW are all set to have smooth ride. Once the booking is done, you will get a message from TempTravel providing the details about the vehicle owner. Meet the vehicle owner, complete the payment process and you are set to go!

RETURN

After your ride, you can return the vehicle to the vehicle owner. Done! Hope you had a great time discovering the new way of efficient travelling. Hope to see you back!

RENT OUT

Done with the registration? Let us move towards renting. Click on RENT to continue smoothly with further process

ORDERS

Once your vehicle is up for public renting, you may start getting orders. Once a user wishes to book your vehicle, you will get a message from TempTravel confirming the booking. You will get the details of the person who has booked your vehicle.

VEHICLE REGISTRATION

You will be directed to a vehicle registration form. Fill the form with correct details and accept the terms and conditions

PAYMENT

You can collect the required amount from the vehicle seeker. The vehicle seeker will then return the vehicle before the allotted time. Done! Hope you had a great time discovering the new way of efficient renting. Hope to see you back!
